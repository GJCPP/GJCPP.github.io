Hi! I am Jiacheng Gao, currently an M.S. student in the School of Computer Science at Nanjing University, China. My research focuses on cryptography, and I am advised by Prof. [Yuan Zhang](https://zynju.github.io/zhangyuan/).

My current research interests center on zero-knowledge proofs (ZKP), including both theoretical foundations and practical implementations and applications. In the summer of 2025, I had the opportunity to work as a research intern in Prof. [Jiaheng Zhang](https://zjhzjh123.github.io/)’s group, where I primarily focused on designing and implementing zero-knowledge proof systems for verifying the training process of machine learning models.

Prior to working on ZKP, I also worked on secure multi-party computation (MPC), with an emphasis on protocol design and performance optimization.
